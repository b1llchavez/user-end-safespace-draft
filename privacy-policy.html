Privacy Policy

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

BM Bill expand_more

Your Privacy Matters

At SafeSpace PH, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Case-related information and documentation
  • Appointment and consultation details
  • Communication records between you and our legal professionals

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide legal assistance and support services
  • To schedule and manage your appointments
  • To maintain case records and documentation
  • To improve our services and user experience

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure storage systems and regular backups
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
